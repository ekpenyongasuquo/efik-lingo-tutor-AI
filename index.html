üöÄ The Solution: A Grounded Reasoning Engine

Unlike generic chatbots that often hallucinate translations, Efik STEM Tutor uses System-Level Grounding to enforce scientific and linguistic accuracy.

Key innovations

  • Linguistic Grounding Layer: curated Efik STEM vocabulary prevents vague or incorrect terms.
  • Vigesimal Math Logic: respects Efik base-20 counting for explanations and calculations.
  • Contextual RAG: explains abstract concepts through local examples (cassava farming, Calabar River, etc.).

Quick demo prompts

1) Explain photosynthesis using cassava and Efik terms. 2) Multiply 20 by 3 using Efik vigesimal logic. 3) Explain gravity using a farm example in Cross River State.
